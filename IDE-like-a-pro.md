## Vim:

- Enter `Insert Mode`:

  - `i`: insert **before** the cursor
  - `I`: insert at the beginning of the line
  - `a`: insert (**a**ppend) after the cursor
  - `A`: insert (**a**ppend) at the end of the line

- Back to `Normal Mode`: `Esc`

- `v`: `V`
- `^v`: `Ctrl + V`
- `V`: `Shift + V`
